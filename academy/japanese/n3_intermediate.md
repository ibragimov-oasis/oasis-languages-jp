## 1. フォーマル表現 / Formal and Written Expressions

### によって (ni yotte) - By/Depending On/According To
**意味**: Means, agent, or variation
**例文**: 
- 人によって意見が違う。(Hito ni yotte iken ga chigau.) - Opinions differ depending on the person.
- この問題は科学技術によって解決できる。(Kono mondai wa kagaku gijutsu ni yotte kaiketsu dekiru.) - This problem can be solved by means of technology.

### に対して (ni taishite) - Towards/In Contrast To
**意味**: Direction, attitude, or contrast
**例文**: 
- 彼は私に対して失礼だ。(Kare wa watashi ni taishite shitsurei da.) - He is rude towards me.
- 日本に対して、アメリカは広い。(Nihon ni taishite, Amerika wa hiroi.) - In contrast to Japan, America is large.

### に関して (ni kanshite) - Regarding/Concerning
**意味**: Concerning, about
**例文**: この件に関して話し合いましょう。(Kono ken ni kanshite hanashiaimashou.) - Let's discuss regarding this matter.

### について (ni tsuite) - About/Concerning
**意味**: About, concerning
**例文**: 日本の歴史について勉強している。(Nihon no rekishi ni tsuite benkyou shite iru.) - I'm studying about Japanese history.

## 2. 原因と結果 / Cause and Effect

### ため(に) (tame ni) - For/In Order To/Because
**意味**: Purpose or reason
**例文**: 
- 試験のために勉強する。(Shiken no tame ni benkyou suru.) - Study for the exam.
- 雨のため試合は中止になった。(Ame no tame shiai wa chuushi ni natta.) - Because of rain, the match was cancelled.

### おかげで (okage de) - Thanks To
**意味**: Positive cause (gratitude)
**例文**: 先生のおかげで合格しました。(Sensei no okage de goukaku shimashita.) - Thanks to my teacher, I passed.

### せいで (sei de) - Because Of (Negative)
**意味**: Negative cause (blame)
**例文**: 雨のせいで遅れた。(Ame no sei de okureta.) - I was late because of the rain.

---

## 3. 程度と比較 / Degree and Comparison

### ば～ほど (ba~hodo) - The More...The More
**意味**: Proportional relationship
**例文**: 
- 勉強すればするほど分かる。(Benkyou sureba suru hodo wakaru.) - The more you study, the more you understand.
- 考えれば考えるほど難しい。(Kangaereba kangaeru hodo muzukashii.) - The more I think, the more difficult it becomes.

### ほど (hodo) - To The Extent/As Much As
**意味**: Degree, extent
**例文**: 
- 死ぬほど忙しい。(Shinu hodo isogashii.) - Busy to death (extremely busy).
- 彼ほど優秀な人はいない。(Kare hodo yuushuu na hito wa inai.) - There's no one as excellent as him.

---

## 4. 時間と順序 / Time and Sequence

### ところ (tokoro) - About To/Just Did/In The Process
**意味**: Point in time relative to action
**例文**: 
- 今から行くところです。(Ima kara iku tokoro desu.) - I'm about to go now.
- 今帰ったところです。(Ima kaetta tokoro desu.) - I just got home now.
- 今食べているところです。(Ima tabete iru tokoro desu.) - I'm eating right now.

### うちに (uchi ni) - While/During/Before
**意味**: Within a time period
**例文**: 
- 若いうちに旅行したい。(Wakai uchi ni ryokou shitai.) - I want to travel while I'm young.
- 忘れないうちにメモしよう。(Wasurenai uchi ni memo shiyou.) - Let me make a note before I forget.

### たびに (tabi ni) - Every Time/Whenever
**意味**: Each occurrence
**例文**: 会うたびに綺麗になる。(Au tabi ni kirei ni naru.) - She gets prettier every time I see her.

### 次第 (shidai) - As Soon As/Depending On
**意味**: Immediately after or dependence
**例文**: 
- 準備ができ次第出発します。(Junbi ga deki shidai shuppatsu shimasu.) - We'll depart as soon as preparations are complete.
- あなた次第です。(Anata shidai desu.) - It depends on you.

---

## 5. 推量と様態 / Conjecture and Appearance

### らしい (rashii) - Seems/Apparently
**意味**: Conjecture based on information
**例文**: 彼は医者らしい。(Kare wa isha rashii.) - He seems to be a doctor.

### ようだ (you da) - It Seems/Looks Like
**意味**: Similarity or conjecture
**例文**: 雨が降るようだ。(Ame ga furu you da.) - It looks like it will rain.

---

## 6. 逆接と対比 / Contrast and Opposition

### のに (noni) - Although/Even Though/Despite
**意味**: Contrary to expectation
**例文**: 
- 勉強したのに合格できなかった。(Benkyou shita noni goukaku dekinakatta.) - Although I studied, I couldn't pass.
- 高いのに美味しくない。(Takai noni oishikunai.) - It's not delicious even though it's expensive.
